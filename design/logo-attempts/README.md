# OpenScout Focused Logo Attempt

One simple SVG direction based on the user's reference: a thin geometric network
mark that serves as the opening "O" in OpenScout, with a small red broker cube at
the route intersection.

## Files

- `focused-01-network-o-lockup.svg` - focused wordmark pass based on the user's hex/network reference.
- `focused-01-network-o-mark.svg` - matching mark-only app/avatar pass.
- `focused-01-network-o-preview.svg` - preview of the focused pass at a few sizes.
- `focused-02-simple-geometry-lockup.svg` - simpler geometric mark with no route lines.
- `focused-02-simple-geometry-mark.svg` - matching mark-only app/avatar pass.
- `focused-02-simple-geometry-preview.svg` - preview of the simple geometric pass.
- `focused-03-wire-cube-lockup.svg` - isometric cube/room mark inspired by the latest reference.
- `focused-03-wire-cube-mark.svg` - matching dark app/avatar pass.
- `focused-03-wire-cube-preview.svg` - preview of the wire cube pass.
- `focused-04-dotted-cube-lockup.svg` - cube mark with dotted outer planes and a clean inner echo.
- `focused-04-dotted-cube-mark.svg` - matching dark app/avatar pass.
- `focused-04-dotted-cube-preview.svg` - preview of the dotted cube pass.

## Concept

The hexagon gives a scout/map/cell shape without turning into an outdoors badge.
The inner lines carry "broker and routes." The small red cube is the local broker
where routes meet.

`focused-02` strips this back further: a precise hex shape with a smaller
line-only inner hex echo, with no internal route diagram.

`focused-03` makes the geometry spatial: an isometric cube frame with a smaller
cube echo inside, closer to the "local room / contained broker" reference.

`focused-04` keeps the same cube geometry but adds SVG dot-pattern planes, closer
to the latest dotted cube reference.
