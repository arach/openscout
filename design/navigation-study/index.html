Scout — Navigation Chrome Study

Three arrangement options for the web app chrome (sidebar · side rail · top row · inspector). Click a card to open the interactive mockup. Companion write-up: docs/design/navigation-study.md

Option A · Recommended

Anchored L

The annotated-mock direction. Full-height sidebar + content-inset top row, refined to read as one system. Smallest delta — polish, not restructure.

 Option B

Full-width Mast

A true full-width top row across everything; the sidebar drops below it and the logo moves into the mast. Needs sign-off (reverses the full-height sidebar).

 Option C

Telescoping Rail

Kills the double rail — nav + context collapse into one 48px strip; context flies out on demand. Most ambitious; sequence after A.
